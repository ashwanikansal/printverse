## ü§ñ How to Use the Website

### 1. Browse Artworks  
Scroll through the gallery and click on any artwork to view its details (price, size, image preview).

### 2. Place an Order  
- Click **Order** inside the artwork modal  
- The chatbot will automatically open  
- Start a conversation with the bot by saying e.g. hi, and then place an order.  
- You can add more items, change quantity, or remove items  
- Confirm the order inside the chatbot to receive an **order ID**

### 3. Track Your Order  
- Open the chatbot and type:  Track my order
- Provide your **order ID**  
- The bot will instantly tell you the current status
